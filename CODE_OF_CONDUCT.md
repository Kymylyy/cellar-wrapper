# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Threatening or abusive language
- Publishing private information without consent

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through repository maintainers using private channels when possible.
For security-related reports, use `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or ban contributors for severe or repeated violations.
